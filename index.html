Please read carefully so as not fill your request form wrongly...

Our company stands out amongst alot, and our employee to management relationship is the number one relationship.
As part of the what companies are to do, leave is one part, and this compnay's policy will help you decide on how
to plan your leave, no matter the type you are requesting for.


Regardless of what your leave request might be about, every employee is entitled to 28 working days, of which
it can be split anyhow you want it, but shouldnt exceed 28days, except in cases of emergencies.

However, the administrator will go thhrough your supervisor in order to grant your request.

2 people or more from ypur department already requested for leave and it happens to fall within the date you also
picked, the leave will be granted based on a reasonable reason or a first come first serve basis.
